Introduction

Provincial Library Balochistan, Quetta and its attached libraries aim
to encourage and support both academic and research activities of
students, researchers, Scholars. These provide a full range of
services that include book loans, online information searching,
reference services, photocopying, access to millions of digital
resources, and research support services. PLB Libraries provide web
accessible information resources, and easy access to advanced learning
facilities. These libraries are managed by br qualified and
experienced staff who are dedicated to provide quality services to its users.
